Types of Recyclable Waste

Cardboard

Cardboard waste can be recycled to create new cardboard products.

Glass

Glass waste can be recycled into new glass containers or used for other purposes.

Metal

Metal waste, such as aluminum and steel, can be recycled and used to create new metal products.

Paper

Paper waste can be recycled and turned into new paper products.

Plastic

Plastic waste can be recycled to produce new plastic products or used for other purposes.

Trash

Trash or non-recyclable waste should be properly disposed of in designated waste bins or landfills.
